Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026  ·  Effective: March 8, 2026

BringBack ("we," "us," or "our") operates the BringBack customer app and the BringBack Runner app (collectively, the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Apps and related services.

By creating an account or using the Apps, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Apps.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

First name, email address, and password when you register. Runners additionally provide payment/payout details for Stripe Connect onboarding.

Delivery information

Delivery address and order details you enter when placing a request.

Payment information

Payment card details are processed directly by Stripe and are never stored on our servers. We receive only a token and last-four-digits confirmation from Stripe.

Usage data

App interactions, feature usage, and crash reports to help us improve the service.

Device information

Push notification token so we can send you order updates. We do not collect precise background location.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process orders and coordinate deliveries between customers and runners
  • To facilitate payments and runner payouts via Stripe
  • To send order status notifications and important service updates
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To improve the Apps through anonymized analytics

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Between users: When a runner accepts your order, they see your first name and delivery address. Customers see the runner's first name and real-time status.
  • Stripe: Payment and payout processing. Governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud functions. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Order history is retained for up to 3 years for financial record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@bringbackapp.com.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), Firebase Authentication for identity management, and Stripe for PCI-compliant payment handling. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to keep your login credentials confidential.

6. Children's Privacy

BringBack is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@bringbackapp.com.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@bringbackapp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for order updates, runner status changes, and important account activity. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings. Disabling notifications will not affect your ability to use the Apps, but you may miss real-time order updates.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by sending a push notification or displaying a notice in the App. Your continued use of the Apps after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
